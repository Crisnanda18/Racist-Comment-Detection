About This Project

This project is a college assignment for the Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning (AIML) course where we learn various AI and ML models to process data. We have reached the point where we need to create an AI that can predict SARA content. We evaluated 5 models and selected the best one as our final model.

We apologize if during the trial process there are results that do not meet your expectations as we are still in the process of further development.
